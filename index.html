About me

Hello there, my name is Sami! I am a recent Master's of Engineering Graduate in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Oregon State University (OSU). My engineering focus ranges digital logic design to high-performance computing (HPC). I am currently working at Los Alamos National Laboratory under the summer Parallel Computing research fellowship. Having previously worked at onsemi as a Digital Design Engineering Intern and as a teaching assistant throughout my time at OSU, I have grown a strong interest at the intersection between hardware and software.

I am passionate about developing computing systems, from hardware emulation to large multiphysics simulations. My specific technical interests include:

  • Computer architecture and hardware emulation
  • FPGA development using Verilog and SystemVerilog
  • High-performance computing and parallel computing optimization
  • Embedded Systems and Firmware Development
