About me

My engineering focus bridges the gap between digital hardware design and high-performance computing (HPC) software. I recently completed my Master of Engineering in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Oregon State University, and my current work centers on parallel computing optimization and hardware-software co-design.

I am passionate about developing robust systems, from emulation and game console hardware to large multiphysics simulations. My specific technical interests include:

  • Computer architecture, CPU design, and hardware emulation
  • FPGA development using Verilog and SystemVerilog
  • High-performance computing and parallel computing optimization
